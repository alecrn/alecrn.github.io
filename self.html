About me

My name is Alexander Rovaughn Newman. Rovaughn is my paternal grandfather’s namesake.

I’m a developer from Boise, Idaho. I own the company Rovaughn LLC, from which I develop websites and take programming contracts.

My interests—which are reflected in this blog—include programming, fitness, music, travel, and philosophy. But who knows what will interest them on a given week anyway?

Freedom of information is compelling to me, which kindles my interest in encryption and networking.

This summer my brother and I are embarking on a bike trip from Boise to Columbus, OH, and then Philadelphia, PA, after which this blog will be updated with the trip journal.
